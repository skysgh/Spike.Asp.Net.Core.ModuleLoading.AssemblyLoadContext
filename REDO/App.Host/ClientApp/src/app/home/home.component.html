Background

Module loading is the basis of providing a 3rd party evolvable ecosystem.
Except that the Asp.Net Core framework didn't think this was something important enough to solve for.
The issue remains that with IServiceCollection having made the IServiceProvider at startup, and the IServiceProvider is locked off from further change...there is nowhere to register a Service that became available when the new module was loaded.
